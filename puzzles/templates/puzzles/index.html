Happy Birthday, Honeybunch!

Welcome to the puzzles app. This is a simple web app designed to help you get to the combination that opens your gift box. There are three digits in the combination, so we have three puzzles for you to solve. Each puzzle will give you a digit of the combination. Once you have all three digits, you can open the box and get your gift!
