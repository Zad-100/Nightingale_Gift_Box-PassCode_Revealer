Welcome to the Crossword Puzzle!

This is the second of the puzzles of the series!! Continuing the momentum from the slider puzzle, this puzzle is a crossword puzzle - Enough with the pictures! 😅

(Note: This crossword isn't exactly "coded" by me. I used an online tool to generate the crossword puzzle. The tool is called "Crossword Labs" and it generated a link to the crossword that I embedded in this page. I simply gave it the questions and their answers and set the orientation stuff 🙂)


Instructions

⚠ So, like any crossword puzzle, you have to fill in the words using the clues given for both 'across' and 'down'. The words are not case-sensitive. So, you can enter the words in any case you want.

☝🏻 Just tap on the grid box that you want to put a letter into.

☝🏻 After you have entered the whole word, hit enter to register the word and move on to the next word.

☝🏻 When you hit enter, if the word is correct, it will be coloured green, else, it will be coloured red, so you know when you have guessed the word wrong.

☝🏻 You can increase the size of the clues window by simply dragging the top of the window upwards.

☝🏻 You can also go to a word from its clue. Simply tap on the clue to go to the word in the grid and start filling it up *That's what she said*

⭐ On solving the crossword puzzle, you will see a star pop out on the crossword. That's the website's way of telling you that you have solved the puzzle correctly.


You go, girl! ✨




If you think you have solved the crossword puzzle, tap on the button below and follow the steps mentioned in the next page.

⚠ But before that - MAKE SURE YOU HAVE THE ANSWERS OF THE CROSSWORD WRITTEN SOMEWHERE. You will need them in the next page. 🙂
